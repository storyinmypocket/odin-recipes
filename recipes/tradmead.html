Recoil-Operated's $12 Traditional Mead

Description

(For HTML demonstration purposes, I'm including the recipe from this Tumblr post. I've formatted it and done some basic edits for grammar and clarity: it's much funnier in the original format.)

So one of the most common things I see on my mead posts is “I’d love to do that, but I don't have the stuff.”

Well, sit down and buckle up, because I'm about to show you how to make a $12.56 traditional mead.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon Deer Park/spring water. You don't want distilled.
  • 3 lb or 32 fluid ounces honey.
  • One packet of yeast.
  • A party balloon.

Steps

  1. Put everything together on a clean work surface. You will need a clean glass, and while not entirely necessary, a measuring cup will be handy.
  2. Pour a cup of water for yourself and drink it. Hydration is important. Also, this will allow you headspace.
  3. Remove about a quart or so of water to drink later.
  4. Wash your drinking cup, and mix about a teaspoon of honey with some water.
  5. Add a packet of yeast to the honey water.
  6. Stick your bottle of honey into some hot water to warm it up.
  7. Go outside. Breathe the free air. Know what it is... to truly live.
  8. Enough of that, bitch. Honey's hot. Put it in the jug of water.
  9. Put some water in the jug of honey, too.
  10. Shake the sin out of it.
  11. Put that stuff (the water and honey in the honey bottle) back in the big bitch (the water jug).
  12. Shake the sh*t out of it.
  13. Hydrate yourself with the water you removed earlier.
  14. Shank a balloon with a pin.
  15. Add your yeasty honey water to the water jug.
  16. Balloon it. (Stretch the neck of the balloon and put it over the opening of the water jug.)
  17. Label it.
  18. If your trad mead says anything racist, or anything positive about Hitler, straighten that sh*t out. (See image at top.)
  19. Stick it somewhere dark and leave it alone for a a while. Shake the hell outta it once a day for the first four days. Then let it be until it's clear.
