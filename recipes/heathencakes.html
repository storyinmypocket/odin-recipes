Heathen Cakes

This is based off a 14th Century German recipe, as recreated on Medieval Cookery by Deni Ameline Dinwiddie and William Codie Dinwiddie.

Makes two pies. Each pie will serve 6-8 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 package center cut bacon, chopped
  • 1 small onion, rough chopped
  • 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 carrot, rough chopped
  • 1 tsp. black pepper, ground
  • 1 stalk celery, rough chopped
  • 2 large apples, diced
  • water
  • 2 pie crusts, pre-baked
  • 1 Tbsp. Italian Herbs (or other herb blend)
  • 1 dozen large eggs, beaten

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, celery, carrot in stock pot with 1 Tbsp salt and herbs and enough water to cover.
  2. Bring to a simmer and cook until meat is fork tender.
  3. Remove meat from stock, and shred.
  4. Cook bacon until crisp.
  5. Remove bacon and let drain on paper towel.
  6. Cook diced apple in bacon fat until tender, about 10 minutes.
  7. Remove apples and drain on paper towel.
  8. Combine beef, apples, bacon, salt, pepper and eggs.
  9. Turn into pre-baked pie crusts.
  10. Bake at 400Â°F for 10-12 minutes, until egg is set.
